Working At SRMPS

Working at SRMPS involves a lot of hard work & commitment coupled with fun and enjoyment. Learning is a two - way process and both students and staff find the culture challenging and stimulating. The desire to stand out and make their presence felt in as brief a time span as possible is very strong amongst the school fraternity.
